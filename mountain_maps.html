Mountain Maps - Jun 21

Maximise your day on the slopes with crowdsourced trail info.


Tech stack included

Ruby on Rails, JS, Mapbox, Turbo, Stimulus, Bootstrap, Heroku, PostgreSQL, Cloudinary

As part of our final project of the bootcamp, I participated building a web application that allows mountain bikers to explore trails in a 3D map.

It was a very challenging project, because of the short time (2 weeks) we had to create a visually appealing and functional web application. We learned from our previous experience as a team building BaguetteDash, this time we had more time related achievable goals, which in my opinion was the key of success of the project.
